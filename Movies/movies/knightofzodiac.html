"Saint Seiya" follows the story of young warriors known as "Saints," who are sworn to protect the goddess Athena and the Earth from evil forces. Each Saint wears a mystical armor called a "Cloth" and possesses unique abilities based on the constellation they represent. The series combines elements of mythology, martial arts, and cosmic battles.
