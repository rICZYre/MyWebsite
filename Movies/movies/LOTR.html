Based on the classic fantasy novel by J.R.R. Tolkien, the trilogy follows the perilous journey of a young hobbit named Frodo Baggins, who embarks on a quest to destroy the One Ring, a powerful artifact that could bring about the destruction of Middle-earth if it falls into the wrong hands. Frodo is joined by a fellowship of diverse companions as they traverse the vast and dangerous lands of Middle-earth, encountering allies and enemies along the way.
