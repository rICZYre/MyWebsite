Release Date: The first season of "The Mandalorian" premiered on Disney+ on November 12, 2019. The second season premiered on October 30, 2020. As of my last update, the series has completed its second season.

"The Mandalorian" follows the journey of a lone Mandalorian bounty hunter, known as Din Djarin, as he navigates the outer reaches of the galaxy in the tumultuous aftermath of the fall of the Galactic Empire and before the emergence of the First Order. Along the way, he encounters various characters and undertakes missions that lead him to unexpected discoveries, including the care of a mysterious Force-sensitive child, known as Grogu or "The Child," whom he affectionately calls "Baby Yoda."
