Released in 2012, "Tao Lang" addresses various societal issues, including poverty, corruption, and social inequality. The song's title translates to "Just Human" in English, emphasizing the shared humanity of all individuals regardless of their background or social status.

OPM Songs

TINGIN ｜ By: Cup of Joe and Janine Teñoso

Tingin is a Filipino song performed by Cup of Joe, a music collective, and Janine Teñoso, a talented singer-songwriter. The track gained popularity in the Philippines for its heartfelt lyrics and soulful melody.

Released in 2019, "Tingin" is a romantic ballad that explores themes of love, longing, and devotion. The song's title translates to "Glance" in English, suggesting the significance of subtle gestures and expressions in expressing affection.

ERE ｜ By: juan karlos

EREis a Filipino song by the artist Juan Karlos Labajo, who is also known by his stage name juan karlos. The track gained popularity in the Philippines, particularly within the local music scene.

Released in 2020, "ERE" is a catchy and upbeat song that showcases juan karlos' musical style, which often blends elements of pop, rock, and alternative music. The title "ERE" is colloquial Filipino slang that translates roughly to "Here" in English.

Bulag, Pipi at Bingi By: Yeng Constantino

Bulag, Pipi at Bingi is a Filipino song performed by Yeng Constantino, one of the most popular and successful Filipino singers and songwriters. The title translates to "Blind, Mute, and Deaf" in English.

Released in 2007 as part of Yeng Constantino's debut album "Salamat," "Bulag, Pipi at Bingi" became an instant hit in the Philippines. The song's poignant lyrics and powerful melody resonated with listeners, earning praise for its emotional depth and social relevance.
