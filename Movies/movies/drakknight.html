Release Date:
"The Dark Knight" was released in the United States on July 18, 2008.

In "The Dark Knight," Batman faces one of his greatest challenges when he encounters the anarchic and unpredictable Joker, who wreaks havoc on Gotham City with a series of escalating crimes. The film explores themes of chaos, morality, and the nature of heroism as Batman and his allies struggle to stop the Joker's reign of terror while facing difficult moral choices.
