"My Hero Academia" is set in a world where nearly everyone possesses superpowers known as "Quirks." The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a Quirkless boy who dreams of becoming a hero like his idol, All Might. Despite lacking powers, Izuku's determination catches the attention of All Might, who passes on his Quirk to him, allowing Izuku to attend U.A. High School, a prestigious academy for aspiring heroes. Alongside his classmates, Izuku trains to become a hero and faces various challenges, including villains and rivalries.

The anime has been praised for its dynamic characters, compelling story arcs, and exciting action sequences. It has become a major success both in Japan and internationally, with a dedicated fanbase and numerous accolades. "My Hero Academia" has also spawned movies, video games, and other media adaptations.
