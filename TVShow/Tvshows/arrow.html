Spoiled billionaire playboy Oliver Queen is missing and presumed dead when his yacht is lost at sea. He returns five years later a changed man, determined to clean up the city as a hooded vigilante armed with a bow.

"Arrow" follows the story of Oliver Queen, a billionaire playboy who becomes the vigilante Green Arrow after being stranded on a deserted island for five years. Upon returning to his hometown of Starling City, Oliver takes on the mantle of the Green Arrow to fight crime and corruption, while also confronting his own demons and navigating complex relationships with his family and allies. The series is part of the Arrowverse, a shared universe of superhero television series based on DC Comics characters.
