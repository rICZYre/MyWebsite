"GI Joe: Retaliation" was released in the United States on March 28, 2013.

In "GI Joe: Retaliation," the elite military unit known as GI Joe faces a new threat when Cobra, a terrorist organization, infiltrates the highest levels of the US government and frames the Joes for treason. Forced to go underground, the surviving members of GI Joe must uncover the truth behind Cobra's plot and stop them from plunging the world into chaos.
