DAREDEVIL

Action
Drama
Crime

Creator:Drew Goddard (initial showrunner), based on the Marvel Comics character created by Stan Lee and Bill Everett

Cast:Charlie Cox as Matt Murdock / Daredevil
Deborah Ann Woll as Karen Page
Elden Henson as Franklin "Foggy" Nelson
Vincent D'Onofrio as Wilson Fisk / Kingpin
Jon Bernthal as Frank Castle / Punisher (season 2)
Élodie Yung as Elektra Natchios (season 2)
Rosario Dawson as Claire Temple

Director:Various directors contributed to the series, including Phil Abraham, Farren Blackburn, and Stephen Surjik, among others.

Release Date:The first season of "Daredevil" premiered on Netflix on April 10, 2015. The series ran for three seasons, with the final season released on October 19, 2018.

"Daredevil" follows the story of Matt Murdock, a blind lawyer by day and vigilante crime-fighter by night, who uses his heightened senses to fight crime in the Hell's Kitchen neighborhood of New York City. As Daredevil, Matt confronts powerful criminal organizations, including Wilson Fisk, also known as the Kingpin, while also grappling with his own moral dilemmas and personal demons. The series is known for its gritty tone, complex characters, and intense action sequences, and it is part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
