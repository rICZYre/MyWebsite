Release Date:
"Grave of the Fireflies" was released in Japan on April 16, 1988.

The film tells the story of Seita and his younger sister Setsuko, who struggle to survive in Japan during the final months of World War II after their home is destroyed in an American firebombing raid. The siblings face starvation, illness, and the indifference of their countrymen as they try to find food and shelter in the midst of the war's devastation.
