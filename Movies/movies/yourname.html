Release Date:
"Your Name" was released in Japan on August 26, 2016.

The film follows the story of two teenagers, Taki Tachibana and Mitsuha Miyamizu, who mysteriously swap bodies. As they experience each other's lives and try to communicate through notes and messages, they develop a deep connection. However, their bond is put to the test when a catastrophic event threatens to tear them apart.
