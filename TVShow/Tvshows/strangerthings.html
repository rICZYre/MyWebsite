Director: Various directors have contributed to the series, including the Duffer Brothers themselves, Shawn Levy, and Andrew Stanton, among others.

Release Date:The first season of "Stranger Things" premiered on Netflix on July 15, 2016. The series has continued with subsequent seasons, with the fourth season expected to premiere in 2022.

"Stranger Things" is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The series begins with the disappearance of a young boy named Will Byers, which leads his friends, family, and local authorities to uncover a series of mysteries involving secret government experiments, supernatural forces, and a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down. As the series progresses, the characters must confront terrifying creatures and face personal challenges while trying to protect their town and each other.
