Created by:The "Dragon Quest" series was created by Yuji Horii, developed by Chunsoft (now known as Spike Chunsoft), and published by Enix (now part of Square Enix).

Cast:

Kentaro Sakaguchi as Prince Harry (Harry in the English version)
Takayuki Yamada as Papas (Pankraz in the English version)

Director: Takashi Yamazaki

Release Date:
"Dragon Quest: Your Story" was released in Japan on August 2, 2019.

The film follows the story of Luca, a young boy who embarks on an epic journey to rescue his mother and defeat the evil Ladja. Along the way, he encounters various allies, faces formidable enemies, and discovers his own destiny as the Hero of Legend.
