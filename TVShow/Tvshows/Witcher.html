Created by:Lauren Schmidt Hissrich

Main Cast:Henry Cavill as Geralt of Rivia
Anya Chalotra as Yennefer of Vengerberg
Freya Allan as Princess Cirilla (Ciri)
Joey Batey as Jaskier (Dandelion)
MyAnna Buring as Tissaia de Vries

Director:Various directors contributed to the series, including Alik Sakharov, Alex Garcia Lopez, and Marc Jobst, among others.

Release Date: The first season of "The Witcher" premiered on Netflix on December 20, 2019. The second season premiered on December 17, 2021.

"The Witcher" follows the story of Geralt of Rivia, a solitary monster hunter known as a Witcher, as he navigates a world filled with dangerous creatures, magic, and political intrigue. Geralt's path becomes intertwined with that of a powerful sorceress named Yennefer of Vengerberg and a young princess named Cirilla (Ciri), who harbors a mysterious power.
