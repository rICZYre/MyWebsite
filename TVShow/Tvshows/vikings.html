Release Date:The first season of "Vikings" premiered on the History Channel on March 3, 2013. The series concluded with its sixth season, which was divided into two parts, airing its final episodes on December 30, 2020.

"Vikings" follows the legendary Norse hero Ragnar Lothbrok and his descendants as they rise to power, explore new lands, and engage in epic battles during the Viking Age. The series explores themes of power, politics, religion, and the clash of cultures as Ragnar and his allies raid and conquer territories, forge alliances, and confront their enemies. "Vikings" is known for its compelling characters, intricate plotlines, and stunning cinematography, and it has garnered critical acclaim for its portrayal of Viking history and culture.
