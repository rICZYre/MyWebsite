Director:Various directors contributed to the series, including Terry McDonough, Breck Eisner, and Jeff Woolnough, among others.

Release Date: The first season of "The Expanse" premiered on Syfy on December 14, 2015. The series continued for five seasons on Syfy before being picked up by Amazon Prime Video for its final three seasons. The final season was released on December 10, 2021.

"The Expanse" is set in a future where humanity has colonized the solar system, and it follows a diverse cast of characters, including a detective, a ship's captain, and a political leader, as they navigate political tensions, interstellar conflict, and mysterious alien technology. The series is known for its intricate world-building, complex characters, and compelling storylines, and it has garnered critical acclaim for its realistic depiction of space travel and its exploration of themes such as politics, identity, and the human condition.
