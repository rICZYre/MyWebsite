Release Date:
"My Hero Academia: Two Heroes" was released in Japan on August 3, 2018.
"My Hero Academia: Two Heroes" premiered in English-language territories later, with the English dub release occurring on September 25, 2018.

The film is set between the events of the second and third seasons of the anime series "My Hero Academia" and follows the students of U.A. High School attending the "I-Expo," a world-class exhibition showcasing the latest hero innovations. During the event, Deku and his classmates encounter Melissa Shield, a girl with a Quirkless status similar to Deku's. However, their enjoyment of the expo is cut short when villains disrupt the event, leading to an intense battle to save the day.
