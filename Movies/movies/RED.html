Release Date:
"RED" was released in the United States on October 15, 2010.

In "RED," Frank Moses, a retired CIA agent, finds himself targeted by a high-tech assassin. He reunites with his former colleagues, known as "RED" (Retired, Extremely Dangerous), to uncover a conspiracy that threatens their lives and the safety of the country. The team embarks on a mission to outsmart their adversaries and prove that they still have what it takes to be operatives.
