Life's To-Do List

Dreams, Goals, and Adventures

As a student, I want to own a house because it feels like the next big step in my life cause having my own house would mean I have a stable place to call home plus, there's the sense of pride that comes with owning something. I imagine walking into my own house, knowing that it's mine, and feeling a sense of accomplishment. It's like saying, "Hey, I did it. I have my own place."


As someone who's always dreamed of having a family of my own, it's about creating a loving and supportive environment where I can share life's joys and challenges with those closest to me.

it's about love. I want to experience the joy of loving and being loved by my partner and children unconditionally. There's something incredibly special about the bond between family members that brings warmth and happiness to everyday life.

Then there's the idea of building a legacy. Having a family means passing down traditions, values, and memories from one generation to the next. It's about creating a sense of belonging and connection that lasts a lifetime.


As someone who's always relied on public transportation or borrowed rides from friends, owning a car would be a game-changer for me. Here's why:

First off, it's all about freedom. Having my own car means I can go wherever I want, whenever I want. No more waiting for the bus or having to coordinate schedules with others. I can just hop in my car and go, whether it's for groceries, a road trip, or just a spontaneous adventure.

Plus, there's the convenience factor. With a car, I don't have to lug heavy bags on the bus or worry about missing the last train home. I can run errands on my own time and make spontaneous trips without having to plan around public transportation schedules.


As someone who loves a good adrenaline rush, the idea of trying the Macau Tower Bungy Jump is just too thrilling to pass up.

First off, it's all about pushing my limits and stepping out of my comfort zone. Standing on the edge of that towering structure, knowing that I'm about to take a leap into the unknown, fills me with excitement and anticipation like nothing else.

Plus, there's the sheer thrill of free-falling through the air at breakneck speed. The rush of wind in my face, the feeling of weightlessness as I plummet towards the groundit's the ultimate adrenaline high that I can't wait to experience.


Visit the Seven Wonders of the World: Explore iconic landmarks like the Great Wall of China, Petra in Jordan, the Colosseum in Italy, Machu Picchu in Peru, Chichen Itza in Mexico, the Taj Mahal in India, and Christ the Redeemer in Brazil.

it's about witnessing the incredible feats of human achievement. These iconic landmarks have stood the test of time, spanning centuries and continents, and each one tells a story of innovation, ingenuity, and cultural significance.


As someone who loves excitement and thrills, skydiving is something I've always wanted to try. It's about experiencing the ultimate adrenaline rush and pushing myself out of my comfort zone in the most exhilarating way possible.

it's about the sheer thrill of freefalling through the sky at speeds of up to 120 miles per hour. The feeling of the wind rushing past me and the sensation of weightlessness as I plummet towards the earth is something I can't even begin to imagine, but I know it would be absolutely exhilarating.
