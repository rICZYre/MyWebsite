"The Spiderwick Chronicles" follows the story of the Grace family, who move into the decrepit Spiderwick Estate and discover a hidden world of magical creatures and dangers within the property. As they uncover the secrets of the estate, the Grace children become embroiled in a battle between good and evil forces, with the fate of both the magical world and their own family hanging in the balance.
