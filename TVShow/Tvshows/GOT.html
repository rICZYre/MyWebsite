Game of Thrones

Fantasy
Adventure
Action

Created by: David Benioff and D.B. Weiss

Main Cast: Sean Bean as Eddard "Ned" Stark
Peter Dinklage as Tyrion Lannister
Emilia Clarke as Daenerys Targaryen
Kit Harington as Jon Snow
Lena Headey as Cersei Lannister
Nikolaj Coster-Waldau as Jaime Lannister

Director:Various directors contributed to the series, including Alan Taylor, David Nutter, and Miguel Sapochnik, among others.

Release Date:The first season of "Game of Thrones" premiered on HBO on April 17, 2011. The series concluded with its eighth season, which aired its final episode on May 19, 2019.

"Game of Thrones" is set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos and follows the power struggles among noble families as they vie for control of the Iron Throne and the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros. The series is known for its complex characters, intricate political intrigue, and epic battles, as well as its themes of power, betrayal, and survival.
