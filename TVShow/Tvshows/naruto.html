Release Date:"Naruto Shippuden" premiered in Japan on TV Tokyo on February 15, 2007. The series concluded on March 23, 2017, after airing a total of 500 episodes

"Naruto Shippuden" is the continuation of the original "Naruto" series and follows Naruto Uzumaki's journey as he grows older and faces new challenges. The series focuses on Naruto's quest to become a powerful ninja, protect his village, and reunite with his friend Sasuke Uchiha. Along the way, Naruto and his allies encounter powerful enemies, uncover dark secrets, and confront their own pasts. "Naruto Shippuden" is known for its action-packed battles, emotional storytelling, and memorable characters, and it remains one of the most popular anime series worldwide.
